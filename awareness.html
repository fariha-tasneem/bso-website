Bangladesh Independence Day

Independence of Bangladesh was declared on 26 March 1971, celebrated as Independence Day, from Pakistan. The Independence Day of Bangladesh is celebrated on 26 March when Sheikh Mujibur Rahman declared the Independence of Bangladesh.

International Mother Language Day

In Bangladesh, February 21st (Ekushey February) is observed as the anniversary of the Language Movement when Bangladeshis fought to preserve the Bengali language against the West Pakistani government - making Bangladesh the first nation to fight for its right to speak its own language. In 1999, UNESCO officially announced February 21st as International Mother Language Day and it has since been adopted to promote and preserve all languages!
